## Research Agenda

I study **multi-agent systems and financial markets** through the lens of  
stochastic processes, mean-field limits, and statistical learning.

My work focuses on bridging:

- microscopic agent behavior  
- mesoscopic interaction structure  
- macroscopic market dynamics  

---

## Areas of Work

### Market Microstructure
Modeling price formation and liquidity in modern markets.

- **Dark Pools & Iceberg Orders**  
  Agent-based LOB model under Poisson arrivals  
  → ergodicity, diffusion limits, hidden liquidity effects  

---

### Mean-Field & Opinion Dynamics
Understanding collective behavior in interacting systems.

- **Confidence-Weighted Opinion Dynamics**  
  Nonlinear mean-field model with endogenous signals  
  → Fokker–Planck approximation, convergence analysis  

- **LLM Agent Interaction Dynamics**  
  Modeling divergence between latent belief and expressed language  
  → controlled interaction protocols, identity drift  

---

### Statistical Learning & Signals
Extracting structure from high-dimensional financial data.

- **Bankruptcy Classification**  
  Imbalanced classification with Lasso + ensemble methods  

- **Litigation & Sentiment Signals**  
  NLP-driven feature construction for equity prediction
